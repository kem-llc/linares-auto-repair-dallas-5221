Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Advanced computer diagnostics to identify and fix check engine lights, performance issues, and more.

Schedule this service

Overview

Modern vehicles rely on complex computer systems. Our technicians use professional-grade scanners to read trouble codes and perform live data analysis. We pinpoint the root cause — from faulty sensors to mechanical failures — and provide a clear repair plan. No guesswork, no unnecessary parts.

What's included

  • Accurate diagnosis saves time and money
  • Improved fuel economy and performance
  • Resolves check engine light and drivability issues
  • Transparent reporting with digital evidence

How it works

1

Code Reading

We connect our diagnostic tool to retrieve all trouble codes and freeze-frame data.

2

System Testing

We perform component tests, voltage checks, and visual inspections to confirm the fault.

3

Repair & Verification

After repairs, we clear codes and test drive to ensure the issue is resolved.

Common questions

Can you diagnose an intermittent problem?

Yes, we use advanced tools that record data over time to catch intermittent faults. We may ask you to leave the car for a day or two.

Will you clear my check engine light without fixing it?

No, we believe in fixing the underlying issue. Clearing codes without repair only masks the problem.
