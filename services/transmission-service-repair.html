Transmission Service & Repair

Fluid changes, filter replacements, and full transmission rebuilds for automatic and manual transmissions.

Overview

Whether you need a simple fluid change or a complete rebuild, our transmission specialists have the expertise to get you back on the road. We service both automatic and manual transmissions, using high-quality fluids and parts. Our rebuilds include a comprehensive warranty for your peace of mind.

What's included

  • Smooth shifting and extended transmission life
  • Prevents costly breakdowns
  • Improved fuel efficiency
  • Warranty-backed rebuilds and repairs

How it works

1

Fluid Check & Inspection

We check fluid level, condition, and look for leaks. A road test helps assess shift quality.

2

Service or Repair Plan

Based on findings, we recommend a flush, filter change, or internal repair. We explain all options.

3

Completion & Test

We perform the service or rebuild, refill with correct fluid, and test drive to confirm proper operation.

Common questions

How often should I change transmission fluid?

Most manufacturers recommend every 30,000-60,000 miles. Check your owner's manual or ask us during your next visit.

Do you rebuild transmissions or only replace them?

We rebuild whenever possible — it's often more cost-effective and allows us to upgrade weak components. We only recommend replacement if the case is damaged.
