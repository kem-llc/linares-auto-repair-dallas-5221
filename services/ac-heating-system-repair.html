AC & Heating System Repair

Air conditioning recharge, compressor replacement, and heater repairs to keep you comfortable year-round.

Schedule this service

Overview

A malfunctioning AC or heater can make driving miserable. Our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair all climate control systems. We check refrigerant levels, compressor function, blend doors, and electrical components. From a simple recharge to a full system overhaul, we restore your comfort quickly.

What's included

  • Fast relief from uncomfortable cabin temperatures
  • Proper refrigerant handling for environmental safety
  • Extended system life with preventive maintenance
  • All-season comfort guaranteed

How it works

1

System Inspection

We check refrigerant pressure, compressor operation, and inspect for leaks using UV dye and electronic detectors.

2

Diagnosis & Quote

We identify the faulty component and provide a fixed-price estimate before any work begins.

3

Repair & Performance Check

We repair or replace parts, evacuate and recharge the system, and verify temperatures meet specs.

Common questions

Why is my AC blowing warm air?

Common causes include low refrigerant, a failing compressor, or a clogged condenser. We'll diagnose the exact issue.

Do you fix heater problems too?

Yes, we repair heating systems including heater cores, blower motors, and thermostat issues. We handle both AC and heat.
