Our story

Family-Owned Auto Repair Serving Dallas Since 2008

To provide honest, high-quality automotive repairs at fair prices while building lasting relationships with our community.

Linares Auto Repair was founded in 2008 by master technician Carlos Linares, who saw a need for trustworthy auto service in the Dallas area. Starting with a small shop and a handful of loyal customers, Carlos built a reputation for meticulous work and transparent communication. Over the years, the business grew as word spread about the team’s dedication to getting the job done right the first time.

Today, Linares Auto Repair is a full-service shop serving thousands of Dallas drivers. We invest in the latest diagnostic technology and ongoing training for our staff to ensure every repair meets the highest standards. Whether you drive a sedan, SUV, or truck, we treat your vehicle as if it were our own. Come experience the difference honesty and skill make.
