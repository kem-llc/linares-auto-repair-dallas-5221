Our story

Family-Owned Auto Repair Serving Dallas Since 2005

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair that keeps our community safe on the road. We believe in transparency, fair pricing, and treating every customer like family.

Linares Auto Repair was founded in 2005 by master technician Carlos Linares, who saw a need for trustworthy auto care in the Dallas Design District. Starting with just two bays and a commitment to doing the job right the first time, Carlos built a reputation for honesty and skill. Word spread quickly, and soon Linares Auto became the go-to shop for neighbors who wanted reliable repairs without the dealer price tag.

Today, we've grown to a full-service facility with eight bays and a team of ASE-certified technicians. But our values haven't changed. We still take the time to explain every repair, provide upfront pricing, and stand behind our work with a solid warranty. Whether you drive a sedan, SUV, or truck, you'll get the same personal attention that made us a Dallas institution.

Values
